Property Care services was done privately by the founders for friends and relatives. Realizing there was lot of potential and need for reliable Property Management services in India and Abroad– Property Care Services was formed. The company was founded in 2015 with a vision to provide transparent, efficient and trustworthy property management solutions to Non Resident Indians (NRIs) and Indians who live away from home.

Our PROPERTY CARE monitoring and inspection service aims at protecting your most valuable investmenton your properties while you are out of India. Keeping regulareye on your property significantly reduces risk of encroachment, unauthorized occupation and use. This service is most economical way to have your property monitored regularly. We visit your properties and inspect it to make sure that it is free from unauthorized use or occupation, and in good condition. We immediately report our findings. We upload you digital photos of inside and outside of your properties to give you piece of mind.

With our analysis and information that we share, we will make sure you make highly informed choice every time.
